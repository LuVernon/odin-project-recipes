Pizza Recipe

This recipe is for a tasty cheese pizza. Whilst we won't be including other toppings, you are completely within your own right to employ a personal twist regarding this classic comfort meal. Crafted upon a pre-baked and store-sourced base, you have the option to create the yummy treat with easily accessible and quick-to-prepare ingredients.

Ingredients

  • Pre-baked pizza base
  • Tomato sauce of choice
  • Shredded mozzarella

Steps

  1. Pre-heat oven to 425°
  2. Spread your tomato sauce evenly across the top of the pizza base
  3. Add your shredded mozzarella to liking, whilst maintaining even coverage
  4. Place pizza on baking tray
  5. Once the oven is at 425°, place your pizza on the middle rack for 10-15 minutes, or until cheese has sufficiently melted
  6. Wait 5-10 minutes before cutting and consuming
