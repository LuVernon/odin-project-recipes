Salad Recipe

This recipe is for a quick to make, healthy and delicious salad. All the ingredients are easy to prepare, and fast to combine.

Ingredients

  • Iceburg Lettuce
  • Tomatos
  • Thin Shredded Cheddar Cheese
  • Spring Onions
  • Paprika

Steps

  1. Wash the iceburg lettuce under clean water
  2. Tear layers of lettuce and place them down in a large bowl
  3. Wash and slice your tomatos and spring onions
  4. Throw the aforementioned tomatos and spring onions into your bowl and finally garnish with the cheddar and paprika
