Left-over Pie Recipe

This recipe is for a tasty left-over pie. Simply using your left-over thanksgiving or christmas meal, you'll be able to create a delicious pie with hardly any new expenditure.

Ingredients

  • Puff Pastry
  • Leftovers

Steps

  1. Pre-heat oven to 425Â°
  2. Lay your puff pastry upon a suitable pie baking tray
  3. Add your left-overs to the inner pie
  4. Cover filling with more pastry, and add even holes to allow heat to escape
  5. Bake for 40 minutes, or until warmed through entirely
  6. Wait 10-15 minutes before eating as food shall be hot
